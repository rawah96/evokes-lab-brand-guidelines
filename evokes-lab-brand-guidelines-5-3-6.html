03 — Clear Space & Minimum Size

Let the mark breathe.

Clear space is measured in X — one unit equals the height of the "L" in the wordmark. No text, imagery, or graphic device may enter this zone, on either lockup.

Clear Space — EN Lockup Min 120px / 24mm

04 — Logo Misuse

What Not To Do.

The mark is fixed, not decorative. These are real examples of the logotype altered in ways that break the system — never apply the mark like this.

✕
Stretched / Distorted Never

Never scale the lockup non-uniformly. Proportions are fixed.

✕
Rotated Never

The mark always sits level. It is never rotated or skewed.

✕
Recolored Never

The logotype is monochrome only — never tinted, gradiented, or filled with a brand accent color.

✕
Low Contrast Never

Never place the mark on a background that doesn't meet clear contrast — legibility is non-negotiable.

✕
Added Effects Never

No shadows, outlines, glows, or bevels — the mark is applied flat, exactly as supplied.

CRAFTED WITH CARE EST. 2018 ✕
Crowded Clear Space Never

Nothing — text, imagery, or graphic devices — may enter the mark's protected clear space.
